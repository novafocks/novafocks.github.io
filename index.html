About Me

My Villainous Backstory (Incomplete)

Life's not fair, is it? You see, I, well I, will never be king. And well, you shall never see the light of another day! Adieu. - Scar, The Lion King

For as long as I can remember, I've been working with computers and technology. Surrounded by piles upon piles of desktops, there was so much to learn and all the time in the world to boot. That's where I gained a strong passion for technology. Even then, though, I didn't have a computer of my own so it was my dream to have a machine of my own. I got my first laptop at around 9, and it was an older Dell Latitude, but combined with Linux Mint it was my own world and mine alone.

Thanks to my two brothers, vintage game consoles were a major part of my life as well. From the Atari 2600 to the Wii, I played it all, and I loved it all. I grew up on Terraria, Garry's Mod, Portal, Super Mario 64, Minecraft, and Super Smash Bros. Melee. I missed quite a few but you get the gist.
